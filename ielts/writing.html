IELTS Writing

Writing Task 1

Bạn nên dành khoảng 20 phút cho task này. Viết ít nhất 150 từ.

Đề bài: The chart below shows the number of tourists visiting a particular Caribbean island between 2010 and 2017. Summarize the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

0 words

Bài mẫu tham khảo

The bar chart illustrates the number of visitors to a Caribbean island over an eight-year period from 2010 to 2017, measured in millions.

Overall, the total number of tourists increased steadily over the period shown. Cruise ship passengers showed the most significant growth, while those staying on the island rose more gradually.

In 2010, the island received around 1 million visitors in total, with the majority (0.7 million) staying on the island and the remainder arriving on cruise ships. By 2017, the total number had risen to 3.5 million, with cruise ship passengers accounting for 2 million of this figure.

The most notable trend was the rapid increase in cruise ship visitors, which surpassed island stayers in 2015. While both categories grew, the gap widened in the final two years, with cruise ship numbers almost doubling those staying on the island by 2017.

Tips for IELTS Writing

Quản lý thời gian

Dành 20 phút cho Task 1 và 40 phút cho Task 2. Đảm bảo viết đủ số từ yêu cầu.

Cấu trúc bài viết

Task 1: Introduction, Overview, Details. Task 2: Introduction, Body paragraphs, Conclusion.

Phân tích biểu đồ

Chọn các đặc điểm chính, so sánh và báo cáo xu hướng thay vì liệt kê tất cả số liệu.
